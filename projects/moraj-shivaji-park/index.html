Two homes per floor. A private lift lobby. The Arabian Sea in every frame — at Mumbai's cultural heart.

The market has already spoken: 750+ walk-ins and half the inventory sold within seven days of launch. One out of two homes sold in Shivaji Park in that window was here.

Common questions
What is the price of a flat at Moraj Shivaji Park?

The published launch rate card starts at 5.19 crore for a 1,077 square foot 3 BHK and 7.79 crore for a 1,442 square foot 3 BHK, both sea-facing. These are the developer's published figures, not estimates.

Are all flats at Moraj Shivaji Park sea-facing?

Yes. The tower is planned with two residences per floor, each sea-facing, with private lift lobbies, so every home in the configuration frames the Arabian Sea.

Which floors get the best sea view at Moraj Shivaji Park?

As a G plus 41 tower at Shivaji Park, higher floors clear the surrounding low-rise fabric for the widest sea and Sea Link view. We provide a per-floor view assessment on request.

Is Moraj Shivaji Park RERA registered?

MahaRERA registration details are available on request and at the MahaRERA portal. Project details are sourced from the developer's July 2026 brochure and are subject to change.
