At One Marina, Marine Lines, a new chapter of rarefied luxury unfolds. The Majesté Collection is an exclusive release of signature residences on the higher echelons of One Marina, the 74-storey architectural landmark by Ashwin Sheth Group & YM Infra rising in the heart of Marine Lines.

The Majesté Collection offers an elevated lifestyle where uninterrupted Arabian Sea panoramas, sweeping Queen’s Necklace views and expansive city skylines become part of everyday living — with premium higher-floor residences, a grand arrival experience and a limited inventory release.
