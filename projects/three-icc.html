The next chapter of Island City Center

Quiet, spacious living at Mumbai's new epicenter — without stepping away from the center of it.

THREE ICC rises within Island City Center, the gated Dadar estate by Bombay Realty, alongside the delivered One ICC, Two ICC and Springs. Three towers shaped by fluid lines, generous proportions and expansive glazing bring a new architectural expression to the precinct.

With thoughtfully planned residences, a dedicated amenities level and a design language rooted in modern restraint, THREE ICC brings balance and ease to central-city living.

Technology & sustainability

Construction

International contractors, aluminium formwork, in-house concrete plant, flat-slab design for efficient spaces, double-glazed façade — with a minimum 5-year waterproofing guarantee and 3-year MEP defects & liability warranty.

Environment

Designed to IGBC Gold standards: rainwater harvesting and recharge, 100% wastewater treated and reused, motion-sensor lighting, organic waste composted on site, and EV charging in the parking levels.

Common questions
What is the price of a flat at Three ICC?

Three ICC's rate card is published on enquiry; no list price is publicly advertised, which is why no price is shown here. We publish the developer's rate card the day we receive it.

What configurations does Three ICC offer?

The towers offer 3 to 5 BHK residences ranging from 1,283 to 2,994 square feet carpet, across three towers of 46 to 67 floors.

Who is the developer of Three ICC?

Three ICC is developed by Bombay Realty, part of the Wadia Group, at Island City Center in Dadar, as a freehold gated estate in Central Mumbai.

Is Three ICC RERA registered?

Yes. Towers A, B and C are registered under MahaRERA numbers PR1171012502563, PR1172022600316 and PR1172022600317, available at the MahaRERA portal.
