Boutique luxury at the park

Two homes per floor. A private lift lobby. The Arabian Sea in every frame — at Mumbai's cultural heart.

Moraj Codename Shivaji Park is a landmark G+41 tower by the Moraj Group, 400 metres from the park that shaped Mumbai's identity. Every residence is a sea-view 3 BHK with a private deck, a 300+ sq.ft private lift lobby, and a triple-height entrance lobby below.

The market has already spoken: 750+ walk-ins and half the inventory sold within seven days of launch. One out of two homes sold in Shivaji Park in that window was here.
