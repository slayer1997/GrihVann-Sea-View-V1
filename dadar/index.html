Dadar West through the sea-view lens

The view arc

Dadar West's sea-facing homes look west and south-west across Mahim Bay toward the Bandra–Worli Sea Link, with the open expanse of Shivaji Park keeping the foreground low and green. It is one of the few Central Mumbai pockets where the arc is genuinely open rather than hemmed in by other towers.

Obstruction risk

The park protection is strongest for the front row along Cadell Road and the park edge. A tower one or two rows back can lose the bay behind a neighbouring parapet below the mid-teens floors. We report, per unit, the exact floor at which the water and the Sea Link come clear.

Orientation & light

West and south-west orientation gives long sunsets over the bay and a steady sea breeze, with the Sea Link catching the light after dark. Monsoon-facing units take the south-west winds, so deck depth and glazing are worth checking.
