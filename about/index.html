GrihVann is a Mumbai sea-facing property advisory built around one rule: every price is published, up front, before you have to ask.

What we do

We curate verified sea-facing flats and apartments for sale across Worli Sea Face, Bandra, Juhu, Versova, Prabhadevi, Marine Drive, Mahalaxmi and Dadar. For every home we list, we publish the full developer rate card, carpet area, applicable charges and a per-floor obstruction report — so you know exactly which floors clear the sea view before you book a viewing.

Why we publish every price

Most sea-facing listings in Mumbai are marked “price on request.” We think that serves the broker, not the buyer. GrihVann publishes rate cards, carpet areas and obstruction data for every live listing, and clearly labels researched market estimates as “indicative” wherever a verified, transacted figure isn’t yet available — rather than presenting an estimate as fact.
