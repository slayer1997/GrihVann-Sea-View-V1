Summary

Worli Sea Face is the benchmark address for a sea view in Mumbai. This report sets out what a flat here costs today — per square foot, by floor band, and by configuration — and frames each number so that GrihVann’s own verified, transacted figures can replace the indicative market ranges as they are confirmed. Every figure below is either a published developer rate card or a clearly labelled Indicative market range; nothing is presented as verified until it is.

How to read this report

  • Published — a figure taken from a developer’s rate card as it appears on a GrihVann listing.
  • Indicative market range — researched from public listing portals (99acres, SquareYards and similar) to show the shape of the market. Not a GrihVann quote.
  • Verified transacted — GrihVann’s own registered-transaction data. These slots are intentionally empty until the numbers are confirmed.

The floor-band premium

The defining feature of Worli is that the same floor plan is worth materially more the higher it sits, because more of the 180° arc comes clear of the front row. Until verified deltas exist, we describe the bands qualitatively.

Floor bandView delivered
Podium – ~10thPartial, road-level; highest obstruction risk
~10th – 30thClears the front row; Sea Link enters frame
30th and aboveNear-180° unobstructed arc

Qualitative until verified per-floor deltas are available.
