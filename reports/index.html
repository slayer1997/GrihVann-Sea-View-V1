Why we publish frameworks before we have every number

Most pricing content in Mumbai real estate is scraped from listing portals and presented as market fact. GrihVann’s reports are built the other way: we publish the structure and method first, label every unverified figure as “indicative,” and upgrade each section to a verified number only once we have transacted data behind it.

Have transacted data to contribute?

If you’ve recently bought or sold a sea-facing flat in Mumbai and are open to sharing anonymised pricing, it helps us verify these reports faster.
