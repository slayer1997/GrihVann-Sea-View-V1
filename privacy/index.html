This policy explains what information GrihVann collects when you use sea-views.com, and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry form on this site, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email address, phone number, and any message or listing you ask about. We also capture the page you enquired from and, where present, ad-campaign identifiers (such as a Google Ads click ID) so we can measure which listings and campaigns are working.

How we use it

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your enquiry, arrange viewings, and share relevant listing or pricing information. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Where it is stored

Enquiry submissions are stored in a private, access-controlled Google Sheet used internally by GrihVann. We may use standard web analytics and advertising tools (such as Google Ads and Google Analytics) to understand site traffic and campaign performance; these tools may set cookies or similar identifiers in your browser.

Cookies

This site may use cookies or browser storage (such as session storage) to remember ad-campaign identifiers for the duration of your visit, so that a submitted enquiry can be correctly attributed. These identifiers are not used to build an advertising profile of you outside this purpose.

Your choices

You can ask us to delete the information you’ve submitted, or to stop contacting you, at any time by messaging WhatsApp or calling +91 92898 40824.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact page.
